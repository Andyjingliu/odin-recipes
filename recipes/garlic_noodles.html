Garlic Noodles

Easy Difficulty
Prep Time 10 mins
Cook Time 10 mins
Total Time 20 mins
Servings 4 servings

Description

This quick and easy garlic noodles recipe is perfect for a simple weeknight dinner. It features perfectly cooked noodles tossed in a rich garlic sauce with a hint of butter and fresh herbs. The combination of crispy garlic and creamy sauce creates a satisfying dish that's ready in under 30 minutes.

Whether you're looking for a quick lunch, a side dish, or a light dinner, this recipe delivers big flavor with minimal effort. The beauty of this dish lies in its simplicity‚Äîjust a few quality ingredients combined with proper technique creates something truly delicious. Serve as is or pair with grilled vegetables and protein for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti noodles
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ¬Ω tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Drain the noodles and add them to the skillet with the garlic butter sauce.
  6. Toss everything together until well coated.
  7. Sprinkle with fresh parsley and serve immediately.

üí° Tips & Notes

  • Garlic Perfection: Don't brown the garlic too much or it will taste bitter. Cook it just until fragrant, about 1 minute over medium heat.
  • Butter Quality: Using high-quality butter makes a big difference in this simple dish. Consider using European-style butter for richer flavor.
  • Pasta Water: Save some pasta water before draining. A splash of starchy pasta water can help create a silky sauce if needed.
  • Add-ins: Customize with red pepper flakes for heat, fresh lemon zest for brightness, or grated Parmesan cheese for richness.
  • Noodle Choice: While spaghetti works great, try with other pasta shapes like linguine, fettuccine, or angel hair for variety.
  • Fresh Herbs: Beyond parsley, experiment with basil, chives, or fresh thyme for different flavor profiles.
